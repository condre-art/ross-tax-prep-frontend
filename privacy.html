Privacy Policy

Our Commitment to Privacy

Ross Tax & Bookkeeping LLC is committed to protecting your privacy and complying with all applicable federal, state, and IRS regulations, including GLBA, HIPAA, and SOC 2 standards. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

Information We Collect

  • Personal identification (name, address, SSN, etc.)
  • Tax and financial data (W-2, 1099, bank info, etc.)
  • Device, usage, and authentication data

How We Use Your Information

  • To prepare and file your tax returns in compliance with IRS and federal law
  • To provide secure access to your account and documents
  • To process bank products and refund transfers (if selected)
  • To comply with legal, audit, and regulatory requirements

Security & Compliance

  • All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Access is restricted by role and audited
  • We never sell or share your data except as required by law or IRS regulations
  • We maintain SOC 2, HIPAA, and GLBA compliance

Your Rights

  • You may request access, correction, or deletion of your data at any time
  • Contact us at privacy@rosstaxprepandbookkeeping.com

Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information

  • We do not sell your personal information to third parties.
  • To opt out of data sharing or request deletion, contact us at the email above.

Disclaimers

  • All services are provided in accordance with federal, state, and IRS guidelines.
