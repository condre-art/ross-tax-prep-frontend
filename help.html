Client User Guide

  1. Register or log in to your Client Portal.
  2. Complete onboarding and upload your tax documents securely.
  3. Track your return status and communicate with your preparer.
  4. Sign required forms electronically and make payments online.

Staff User Guide

  1. Log in to the Admin Console for management tools.
  2. Review client submissions, manage returns, and monitor compliance.
  3. Access audit logs, analytics, and training resources.
  4. Use the Onboarding page for new client intake.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I reset my password? Use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page or contact support.
  • Is my data secure? Yes, all data is encrypted and compliant with IRS and GLBA standards.
  • How do I upload documents? Log in to your portal and use the document upload section.
  • How do I contact my preparer? Use the secure messaging feature in your portal.
