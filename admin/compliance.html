Representation Rights Disclosure (IRS Circular 230)

An unenrolled tax return preparer may represent taxpayers before the Internal Revenue Service only for clients whose returns the preparer prepared and signed. Representation is limited to examinations conducted by IRS Revenue Agents and Customer Service Representatives. This status does not authorize representation before Appeals, Revenue Officers, or Counsel.
