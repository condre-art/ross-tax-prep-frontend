## üîí Security Best Practices

1. **Never share your password** with anyone, including IT support
2. **Change your password regularly** (every 90 days recommended)
3. **Use a unique password** not used for other accounts
4. **Enable 2FA** (Two-Factor Authentication) for additional security
5. **Log out** when leaving your workstation
6. **Report suspicious activity** immediately to your manager

## ‚öñÔ∏è Compliance & Privacy

### GLBA Compliance
All employees must complete GLBA (Gramm-Leach-Bliley Act) training within 7 days of hire.

### IRS Circular 230
ERO staff must acknowledge IRS Circular 230 requirements and maintain:
- PTIN (Preparer Tax Identification Number)
- EFIN (Electronic Filing Identification Number)
- Annual continuing education requirements

### Data Privacy
- Never access client data unless required for your job
- Do not share client information outside the organization
- Follow document retention and destruction policies
- Report any data breaches immediately
