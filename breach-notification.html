Breach Notification Policy

Our Commitment to Security

Ross Tax & Bookkeeping LLC is committed to protecting your data and complying with all federal, state, and IRS regulations regarding data breaches and incident response.

What is a Data Breach?

A data breach is any unauthorized access, disclosure, or loss of personal, tax, or financial information.

Notification Procedures

  • We will promptly investigate any suspected or confirmed breach.
  • Impacted individuals will be notified without unreasonable delay, as required by law.
  • Notifications will include the nature of the breach, data involved, steps taken, and recommended actions.
  • We will notify the IRS, state agencies, and law enforcement as required.

Prevention & Response

  • We maintain technical, administrative, and physical safeguards to prevent breaches.
  • All staff are trained in incident response and privacy best practices.
  • We regularly review and update our security protocols.

Contact

If you suspect a breach or have questions, contact us at security@rosstaxprepandbookkeeping.com.
